──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5. Usage in project:
   - Obstacle and enemy models are loaded via Resources.Load<GameObject>("PenguinRun/Models/<name>")
   - Boss silhouettes use procedural geometry (no external models currently)
   - Texture pack applied via TintModel() at runtime; no separate texture import needed for obstacles
